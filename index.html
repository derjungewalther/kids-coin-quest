How it works

  1. 1

    Parents set up quests

    "Feed the dog · 2 🪙" or "Do homework · 5 🪙". You decide the tasks, the coin value, and the weekly adventure rhythm.

  2. 2

    Kids complete quests & grow heroes

    Every completed chore earns coins and XP for their chosen hero. Stats rise, gear unlocks, the character comes to life.

  3. 3

    Go on adventures — earn real treasure

    Once a week the party takes on a guided adventure: 8 scenes of story, dice, and puzzles. Victory drops real coin into their purse.
